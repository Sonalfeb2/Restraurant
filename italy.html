ABOUT

US

LoveItaly was founded in 2014 by LVenture Group, a holding company quoted on the Italian Stock Market that participates in digital start-up businesses, and a group of enthusiasts from diverse professional backgrounds who are passionate about Italy’s cultural heritage and seek to make the conservation of Italy’s outstanding artistic treasures their main focus. The initial font of inspiration came from the archaeological and conservation consultancy work of Richard Hodges, one of the founding Members of LoveItaly, President of the American University of Rome, an Executive Committee Member of the Herculaneum Conservation Project and Counsel to the Packard Humanities Institute.
